Privacy Policy — Sojourner: Been Stay Tracker

Last updated: June 9, 2026

1. Overview

Sojourner ("the app") is a personal travel tracker that helps you log and analyze your stays across countries. Your privacy matters — the app is designed to keep your travel data on your device.

2. Data You Enter

All stays (countries, dates, notes) are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. This data is never uploaded to any server and is not accessible to the developer.

You can export your stays to a CSV file and re-import them at any time. This export is initiated by you, saved locally, and is not transmitted anywhere by the app.

3. Location Data

The app requests approximate location (GPS/network) only when you use the Fast Check-In feature to auto-detect your current country. Location is used in the moment to resolve a country code and is not stored, not logged, and not shared with any third party. You can always add stays manually without granting location permission.

4. Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Screens viewed and features used
  • Country codes from stays added (to understand popular destinations)
  • App settings changes (theme, language)
  • In-app purchase events
  • Screenshot events — on Android 14 and above, when you take a screenshot, the name of the current screen is logged (e.g. "stays", "stats"). The screenshot image itself is never captured or transmitted.

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out via Android's per-app data controls or under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

5. Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) to collect anonymous crash reports. These include device model, OS version, and a stack trace of the error. No personal data or travel data is included.

6. Feedback

When you send feedback via the Help screen, the app opens your email client with a pre-filled message. This message automatically includes:

  • App version
  • Android version and API level
  • In-app language setting

This information is sent directly to sojourner.support@gmail.com and is used only to respond to your inquiry and diagnose issues. It is not shared with any third party.

7. Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing (Google LLC). The app does not collect or store payment information. Purchase status (free/premium) is verified through Google Play and stored locally on your device.

9. Data Retention

  • Local data (stays): kept on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.
  • Analytics & crash data: retained by Google per their standard policies (up to 14 months for analytics).
  • Feedback emails: retained only as long as needed to resolve your inquiry.

10. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
